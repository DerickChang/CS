Mirrorless Interchangeable Camera

Mirrorless Interchangeable is the most recent improvement of digital camera. It has a interchangeable lens, enabling it to adapt to different situation and create different type of photos. Unlike the usual professional camera, it does not have the mirror box installed in the camera and the new electronic viewfinder has replaced the traditional optical viewfinder. This enables the camera to be decrease in size, although still larger than an average point-and-shoot camera; it also saves time of shooting as it does not require people to put down their camera each time to review the photos.
