Future Trend

Digital cameras are expected to emerge new technologies in the future

  • Augmented Reality (AR)
  • Virtual Reality (VR)

Application of digital camera is expected to be broadened to engage in the fields of:

  • Medical imaging
  • Autonomous vehicles
  • Robotics and automation

More high-tech digital cameras equipped with

  • Advanced image sensors
  • More powerful lenses
  • Quicker autofocus

Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

  • Smart scene recognition
  • Automatic subject tracking
  • Real-time image enhancement
  • Advanced post-processing capabilities

Advancement of lens technology

  • New types of lens
    • Liquid lens - a lens that contains a sealed cell with water and oil
    • Adaptive lens - a lens that changes its shape and properties according to the environment and the user's preferences (similar to photochromic lens of eyeglasses!)
  • Advancements
    • High capability in autofocus
    • Resistance to shock and vibration
    • More functionality for different applications
  • Challenges
    • Limited optical quality
    • Low temperature sensitivity
    • Not compatible with other lens elements
    • Complex development process
    • High cost

Transformation of optical process - Computational Photography

  • Replace the traditional optical process
  • Uses digital computation such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, algorithms, to capture images

Question

How do you think these new technologies will impact the creative process of capturing images?
